# Module 2: Compliance-First Operations

## 1.0 Strategic Mandate

This is the most critical module in the Insurance OS. In the 2024-2025 landscape, compliance is not a back-office administrative task; it is the central, non-negotiable strategic foundation of the entire agency.

The "buy-and-dial" era is over. The modern agency operates in a **zero-tolerance regulatory environment**. Failure to build on a "Compliance-First" chassis will result in agency-ending fines, license revocation, and carrier termination.

The goal of this module is to build a **defensible, audit-ready agency** where compliance is an automated, integrated, and non-negotiable part of the daily workflow.

## 2.0 Core Compliance Pillars

This system is built to manage the five key areas of regulatory risk.

* **[TCPA (Calling & Texting)](tcpa/):** Governs all outbound contact. The new "One-to-One Consent" rule is the single greatest threat to traditional lead generation models.
* **[CMS (Medicare & ACA)](cms/):** The "10-Year Mandates" for recording all sales calls and storing all consent documentation are non-negotiable for any agent in the health market.
* **[NAIC (Suitability & Best Interest)](naic/):** Governs product recommendations, especially for IUL and annuities. Requires a documented, defensible process to prove the recommendation is in the client's best interest.
* **[HIPAA & Data Privacy)](hipaa/):** Governs the storage and handling of Protected Health Information (PHI) and Personal Identifiable Information (PII). A data breach is an existential risk.
* **[DOL (Fiduciary Rule)](dol/):** Governs advice related to 401(k) rollovers and qualified funds, primarily impacting annuity and investment-related sales.
