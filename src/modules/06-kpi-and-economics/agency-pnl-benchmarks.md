# Playbook: Agency P&L Benchmarks

## 1.0 Strategic Mandate

This playbook defines "what good looks like" for the agency's business health at three distinct stages. It provides a framework for managing expenses and scaling operations profitably. The primary goal is to manage the **3 Core Expenses:** Leads, Technology, and People (commissions/staff).

## 2.0 P&L Benchmarks by Stage

### Stage 1: The "Solo Agent"

* **Description:** A single producer focused on personal production.
* **P&L Focus:** Maximize Net Commission.
* **Benchmark P&L:**
    * **Gross Commission:** $150,000
    * **Expenses:**
        * Leads: $30,000 (20%)
        * Tech (CRM/Dialer): $3,600 (2.4%)
        * E&O/Fees: $2,400 (1.6%)
    * **Total Expenses:** $36,000 (24%)
    * **Net Income (to agent):** **$114,000 (76%)**
    *

### Stage 2: The "Growing Agency"

* **Description:** 3-5 agents. Owner is a player-coach, still selling but focused on training.
* **P&L Focus:** Manage lead costs and build override income.
* **Benchmark P&L:**
    * **Gross Commission:** $700,000
    * **Expenses:**
        * Commissions (to agents): $270,000 (38.5%)
        * Leads: $140,000 (20%)
        * Tech Stack: $12,000 (1.7%)
        * Admin (Part-Time): $28,000 (4%)
    * **Total Expenses:** $450,000 (64.2%)
    * **Net Income (to owner):** **$250,000 (35.7%)**
    *

### Stage 3: The "Enterprise Agency"

* **Description:** 10-20+ agents. Owner is a full-time CEO focused on systems and recruiting.
* **P&L Focus:** Maximize EBITDA by scaling overrides and managing fixed costs.
* **Benchmark P&L:**
    * **Gross Commission:** $3,000,000
    * **Expenses:**
        * Commissions & Overrides: $1,650,000 (55%)
        * Leads (Co-op): $450,000 (15%)
        * Staff Salaries (Admin, Ops): $180,000 (6%)
        * Tech & Fixed Costs: $120,000 (4%)
    * **Total Expenses:** $2,400,000 (80%)
    * **Net Income (EBITDA):** **$600,000 (20%)**
    *
